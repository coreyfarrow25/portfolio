Founding story

2019
Founded in Paris by Alexis Barreyat (ex-GoPro) and Kévin Perreau. Mission: end curated social media.
2021
Series A · college ambassador program drives US growth.
2022
Viral breakout. 35M downloads in Q3. Apple App of the Year.
2023
Growth stalls. Downloads fall to 5M/quarter. No monetization path.
2024
Acquired by Voodoo for ~$537M. Launches RealBrands/RealPeople feed.
2025
In-feed ads launch. First real revenue: $30M+.

Growth strategy

College ambassador program
Key US growth driver (2021–2022). Students promote BeReal on campuses — how the app escaped France.
RealPeople / RealBrands
Launched Jan 2024. Opens platform to celebrities and brands — attempt to reignite interest after stall.
Expand beyond Gen Z
Only 8% of users are 28+. Growing the older demographic is a major untapped MAU lever.
US market deepening
US is only 5.4M MAUs vs. 40M global. Strong Japan/France presence — US remains the big prize.
